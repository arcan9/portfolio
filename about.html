Hi, I'm Ashelle (prounounced uh-shell), illustrator turned web developer. Like many people, I've dabbled a bit with coding when I was a teen. Tweaking HTML and CSS here and there on my Myspace layout and Tumblr blogs (remember those times?). For years, I worked as a digital artist, illustrating custom art for clients and sold my art at conventions. It really wasn't until early 2022 that I was inspired to get into web developement when I took coding workshops out of curiousity. These workshops seemed like the perfect opportunity to learn a few tech skills in a dip-your-toes manner. It's safe to say that I was hooked by the first few weeks of learning about HTML, CSS, Javascripts, APIs, and the like. Every day I continue to be excited about tech.

As I've worked independently throughout my career, I've always aimed to do work with dogged determination and self-direction. That said, I also enjoy working with others whenever interesing group-oriented projects land my way. A team that places importance on diversity, curiosity, and friendly open communication is something I seek most in my professional life!
