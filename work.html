Projects

Gastronomie

Website that recommends books about food using the Google Books API. If you're a food lover who's looking to find out more about the latest treats from the streets around the world, learn holiday recipes to prepare for the family, or you're simply curious about food in the context of modern and historical culture, Gastronomie will help you satiate your apetite.

Hada Vocab Quiz

A small web app that tests your knowledge of Korean words and keeps score.

Weatherin

A weather forecast application projecting the current weather at your chosen location, as well as weather for the next five days. Works for approximately 200,000 cities around the world. Uses the OpenWeather API.
